About Tilly-Tracker

Using This App


This full-stack Django Web App allows users to make a profile, keep track of their air plants, and different objects you can mount your plants on. You can also keep track how often you water your air plants by misting or soaking and if you gave them fertilizer.

About Air Plants


Air Plants belong to the genus Tillandsia. Like their close cousins the Bromeliads, they often grow epiphyitcally, meaning attached to other plants. With their minimal care needs and ability to be mounted on nearly anything, they have become a staple for indoor gardens and beautifying even the smallest of spaces.
